InstaLess

A minimal, time limited Instagram browser in one lightweight Android app.

Why You'll Love It

Enjoy Instagram without getting stuck scrolling endlessly! Our Kotlin based Android app features a built-in WebView that keeps you logged in, automatically ends your session after a set time, and enforces a cooldown before you can jump back in. It’s perfect for helping you focus your day while still enjoying your social feed.

Stay Logged In

Persistent cookie management keeps your Instagram session active, no need to re-enter credentials every time.

15 Minute Timer

Automatically closes after 15 minutes of browsing to promote healthier usage.

2 Minute Cooldown

Forces a quick break by blocking relaunch within 2 minutes of closure.

Ultra Lightweight

ProGuard/R8 optimization ensures a small APK size, faster downloads and minimal storage usage.

Quick Overview

  • Platform: Kotlin based Android app (API 28+)
  • Core Functionality: Embeds Instagram in a secure WebView
  • Session Handling: Auto saves login cookies for seamless re-launch
  • Timer & Cooldown: 15minute usage limit, 2minute break
  • Minified: ProGuard/R8 for code and resource shrinking

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does this work like the normal Instagram app?
A: It’s more like an embedded browser dedicated to Instagram. You can still log in, post, or browse as usual—but with a time managed approach.
Q: Can I change the 15minute limit or 2minute cooldown?
A: Yes, if you’re comfortable modifying the source code. Otherwise, the time limits are fixed.
Q: Is this available in official app stores?
A: That depends on future release plans. Currently, you can install via the provided APK link or build from source.
